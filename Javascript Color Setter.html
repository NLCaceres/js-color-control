A Little About Me

I'm Nicholas Lionel Caceres, but most people just call me Nick. I'm nearly 23 years old (only a month to go!) and come from a huge Hispanic family. My dad's half of the family is Peruvian while my mom's family is mexican. Though my dad only has one sister giving me one aunt from my dad's side, my mom, on the other hand, actually has eight siblings. Thanks to those eight aunts and uncles, I fortunately grew up with tons of cousins that are like my brothers and sisters despite being the only kid in my entire family to not have a sibling. My family is and always has been such a huge part of my life, and I'm incredibly thankful to have them.

In my own personal life, I'm glad I have a wonderful girlfriend of three years who sticks by my side as well as three amazing lifelong friends that I've known since I was only five years old. My girlfriend is Kat, and we've been fortunate enough to have seen quite a bit of the country together. Just this past summer we saved up all the money we could make, and drove from Irvine, CA straight down the 10 freeway to Disney World near Orlando, Florida. We had plenty of bumps along the road in my old '03 Corolla, but we somehow made the trip in a little over two days! As for those three best buds of mine, they're Antonio, Edwin and Jean, and we've had plenty of our own adventures over the course of 18 years. Whether with my girlfriend or my best pals, I can't help but look forward to many more adventures to come.

As for my School Life

At school, I'm a super-senior, having finished most of my major requirements already. Luckily USC allowed me to finish the financial aid they gave me, so I can take extra classes, finish my minors and take every opportunity to learn here at USC. Here at USC I've taken up a B.S. in Biological Sciences and two fun minors, namely East Asian Languages and Cultures, emphasizing Chinese, and ITP, emphasizing Mobile App Development. Though I'm relatively new to the Web Development world, I thought this class would be a great way to toss myself into the Web Dev world and get a better understanding of programming as a whole. From here, I hope to gain better coding practices, better skills, a deeper appreciation for the APIs I need in Mobile Development, and just to have a lot of fun making web content.
